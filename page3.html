Basically, audio waves are modified through analog or digital methods so that they sound a certain way. A real science-y way to make music which you can find in many genres!

Modular synthesizers are composed of separate modules for different functions. I have used some basic module functions from the p5.js sound library such as:

  • Oscillators
  • Filters
  • Envelopes
  • Sequencers

Tip: Start with a Beat Length of 0, Speed of 600 and Frequency of 1050 Hz to produce a beat that sounds like a horse running!
